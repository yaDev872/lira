Our Programs

Junior Coders

Perfect for ages 7-10. We introduce logic and creativity using Scratch and block-based programming. Kids build their first games and animations.

For ages 11-15. Hands-on construction using Arduino and LEGO Mindstorms. Students learn mechanics, sensors, and automation.
